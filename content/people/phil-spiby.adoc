== Senior Consultant, Eurostep Limited

Phil Spiby is a founding member of the EXPRESS Language Foundation and Senior
Consultant at Eurostep Limited, where he has worked since 1995. He specializes
in PLM, secured collaboration, STEP standards, and the management of large
integration projects.

== Contributions

Phil has been deeply involved in the STEP and EXPRESS community for over three
decades. At Eurostep, he currently works closely with clients to implement full-scope
PLCSlib implementations, from database table structures to client-specific PLCS
templates flowing over an Enterprise Service Bus (ESB).

He was a key contributor to the OASIS Product Life Cycle Support (PLCS) standard
(Version 1.0, 2013), which establishes structured data exchange and sharing
capabilities for complex engineered assets throughout their total life cycle,
based upon ISO 10303 (STEP) Application Protocol 239.

Phil has contributed to major European collaborative projects, including:

* *CONGA (Configuration Optimisation of Next Generation Aircraft)* — ongoing
* *CRESCENDO* (2011–2013) — led Eurostep's contribution to the Behavioural
  Digital Aircraft collaboration hub
