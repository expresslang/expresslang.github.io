== EXPRESS Standards Contributor

Phil Spiby is a founding member of the EXPRESS Language Foundation and a contributor
to the development and application of EXPRESS language standards.

== Contributions

Spiby has been involved in the STEP and EXPRESS community, contributing to the
practical application of EXPRESS in industrial settings and helping bridge the
gap between the language specification and real-world implementations.
