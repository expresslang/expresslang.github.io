== Project Leader, ISO 10303-11:1994

Prof. Bernd Wenzel is a founding member of the EXPRESS Language Foundation and served
as co-project leader of the first edition of the EXPRESS language standard (ISO 10303-11:1994),
together with Douglas Schenck.

== Contributions

Wenzel guided the EXPRESS language through its initial standardization process at ISO,
helping establish EXPRESS as the foundational information modelling language for
industrial data exchange within ISO 10303 (STEP).
