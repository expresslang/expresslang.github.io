== Associate Technical Fellow, Boeing

Stuart Galt is an Associate Technical Fellow at Boeing, specializing in Enterprise
Systems and Business Architecture. He brings more than 25 years of experience working
with visualization and data exchange standards and formats.

== Contributions to Standards

Stuart is an active contributor across multiple ISO technical committees and
industry standards bodies:

* *Convenor of WG 12* at ISO/TC 184/SC 4 — the working group responsible for STEP
  modeling and the STEP Module and Application Protocol (SMRL) validation
* *Project Lead* of ISO/CD 14739-1, _Document management — 3D use of Product
  Representation Compact (PRC) format — Part 1: PRC 10001_, in ISO/TC 171/SC 2
* *Chair* of OASIS Standard WebCGM 2.1
* *Member* of WG 16 (Visualization) and WG 21 (SMRL validation) in ISO/TC 184/SC 4
* *Convener* of TC 171/SC 2 WG 7 (Engineering)
* *Co-chair* of the LOTAR 3D mechanical CAD and PMI group

== Impact

Stuart's work bridges the worlds of 3D visualization, product data exchange,
and document management. His contributions to STEP (ISO 10303), PRC (ISO 14739),
and WebCGM standards have helped enable interoperability across the aerospace
and manufacturing industries.
