== President, EXPRESS Language Foundation

Thomas (Tom) Thurman is President of the EXPRESS Language Foundation and owner
of TRThurman Consulting, specializing in engineering data integration using
STEP Application Protocols.

== Current Roles

* *President*, EXPRESS Language Foundation (since 2023)
* *Owner*, TRThurman Consulting (since 2008) — consulting focused on
  development and implementation of Electronic/Electrical applications of
  ISO 10303
* *Consultant*, The University of Iowa (since 2008) — providing technical
  direction to research programs for modeling and simulating electromechanical
  systems

== Awards

* *ISO Excellence Award* — in recognition of technical leadership, continued
  innovation and personal dedication to the development of ISO 10303
* *PDES, Inc. Bryan K. Martin Technical Excellence Award* — for technical
  contributions and leadership

== Career at Rockwell Collins

Tom spent 40 years at Rockwell Collins (1968–2008) as Principal Electrical
Engineer in the Advanced Manufacturing Technology department. He was
responsible for requirements analysis, activity and information model
development of ISO 10303 standards.

He was the technical lead in the development of all four editions of
ISO 10303-210 (AP 210), the electromechanical application protocol. He
designed system architecture for a Printed Wiring Assembly design analysis
system using AP 210 as the integration basis model, which evaluated several
thousand designs in production use at Rockwell Collins.

== Contributions to Standards

* Technical lead for ISO 10303-210 (AP 210), all four editions
* Co-author: "Research Results and Recommendations for Universally Unique
  Identifiers in Product Data Standards" (NIST AMS 300-12, 2024)
* Led the PREVIEW project for AP 210 design engineering data integration
