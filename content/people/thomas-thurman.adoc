== President, EXPRESS Language Foundation

Thomas Thurman is a founding member and current President of the EXPRESS Language
Foundation, providing strategic leadership for the organization's mission to maintain
and promote the EXPRESS language family.

== Contributions

As President, Thurman oversees the foundation's activities in education,
standardization, research, and promotion of the EXPRESS languages. He was instrumental
in establishing ELF as a registered 501(c)(3) public charity in the United States in
January 2022.

Thurman has been active in the EXPRESS and STEP communities, contributing to
the development and adoption of ISO 10303 standards across industries.
