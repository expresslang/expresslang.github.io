== General Manager, PDES, Inc.

Allison Barnard Feeney is the General Manager of PDES, Inc., an international
industry, government, and university consortium committed to accelerating the
development and implementation of standards for product data exchange in the
Digital Enterprise.

== Career at NIST

Allison served as a mechanical engineer in the Smart Connected Systems Division
of the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST). She addressed
national problems related to the digitalization of manufacturing, including
system interoperability, formal language development, building trust in digital
data, and building knowledge from disparate data sources.

She previously led the Smart Manufacturing Operations Planning and Control
Program in NIST's Engineering Lab, including its Digital Thread for Smart
Manufacturing Project and the Product Definition for Smart Manufacturing
Project in the Model-Based Enterprise program.

== Contributions to Standards

Allison has worked in manufacturing standards development, implementation,
conformance testing, product data standards, and systems integration throughout
her career. She has been a key participant in the development of the STEP
product data standard (ISO 10303) since its inception.

She served as Chair of the US Technical Advisory Group to ISO TC 184/SC 4
and as the US representative to SC 4's Policy and Planning Committee.
