== General Manager, PDES, Inc.

Allison Barnard Feeney is the General Manager of PDES, Inc., an international
industry, government, and university consortium committed to accelerating the
development and implementation of standards for product data exchange in the
Digital Enterprise.

== Previous Roles

* *Mechanical Engineer*, Smart Connected Systems Division, NIST Communications
  Technology Laboratory — addressed national problems in manufacturing
  digitalization, system interoperability, and formal language development
* *Lead*, Smart Manufacturing Operations Planning and Control Program, NIST
  Engineering Lab — including the Digital Thread and Product Definition for
  Smart Manufacturing projects
* *Chair*, US Technical Advisory Group to ISO TC 184/SC 4
* *US Representative*, SC 4 Policy and Planning Committee

== Contributions to Standards

Allison has been a key participant in the development of the STEP product data
standard (ISO 10303) since its inception. She worked throughout her NIST career
in manufacturing standards development, implementation, conformance testing,
product data standards, and systems integration.
