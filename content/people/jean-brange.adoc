== Président, AFNeT Services

Jean Brangé is a founding member of the EXPRESS Language Foundation and
Président of AFNeT Services, a French organization focused on digital
interoperability standards for industry.

== Current Roles

* *Président*, AFNeT Services (since 2021) — SaaS solutions for
  standards-based interoperability
* Leads the *EasySTEP* and *STEP Next* initiatives:
** A universal, free, and open-source STEP viewer (Apache 2.0)
** Full support for STEP formats (AP242, AP209, AP210, PLCS)
** Community-driven vision with no vendor lock-in
* Leads the *TDP Message standardization project* for European Aerospace and
  Defence

== Previous Roles

* *Associé*, Boost SAS (2004–2021)
* *Directeur*, AFNeT (2000–2011)
* *Enseignant*, École Spéciale d'Architecture (1999–2007)
* *Computer Simulations*, RFR (1992–1995)

== Background

With a background in architecture and mathematics, Jean brings a unique
perspective bridging building design and industrial data exchange. He is a
leading figure in the STEP community in Europe, particularly around STEP
AP242 and long-term digital archiving (LOTAR).

His specialties include PLM, secured collaboration, management of large
projects, simulations, STEP, and ISO 8000.
