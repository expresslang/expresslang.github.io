== Vice President of Product Development, STEP Tools, Inc.

Dr. David Loffredo is a co-founder of STEP Tools, Inc. and Vice President of
Product Development. He has led over twenty major releases of the STEP Tools
software, now deployed in over a million CAD seats and used in manufacturing
over a million aerospace parts per year since 2019.

== Contributions to Standards

David is the Editor of ISO 10303-238 (STEP-NC) and the Part 21 STEP file
exchange format. He serves as Convener of ISO TC 184/SC 4 Working Group 11,
overseeing STEP file formats, the EXPRESS modeling language, and programming
language bindings.

He co-led the second edition of the EXPRESS language standard (ISO 10303-11:2004)
together with Peter Wilson, incorporating lessons learned from a decade of
industrial use and clarifying ambiguities in the original specification.

== Technical Work

David has developed core engineering software for managing and meshing ISO 10303
STEP CAD geometry and IFC BIM models, driving CNCs for machining with STEP-NC,
and Digital Twin manufacturing. He is experienced with C, C++, building native
extensions for .NET and Python, Perl, and Ansible.

He is the author of numerous technical papers, articles, training courses, and
web content on CAD geometry and data exchange.
