== Project Leader, ISO 10303-11:2004

David Loffredo is a founding member of the EXPRESS Language Foundation and served
as co-project leader of the second edition of the EXPRESS language standard
(ISO 10303-11:2004), together with Peter Wilson.

== Contributions

Loffredo has been a long-time contributor to EXPRESS standardization and STEP
implementations, bringing deep expertise in both the language specification and
its practical application in industrial data exchange.

He co-led the effort to update the EXPRESS language standard to its second edition,
incorporating lessons learned from a decade of industrial use and clarifying
ambiguities in the original specification.
