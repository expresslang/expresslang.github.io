== Vice-President, EXPRESS Language Foundation

Hiroshi Murayama is a founding member and current Vice-President of the EXPRESS
Language Foundation. He is CEO of SATS Inc. (Standard Applied Technologies and
Services), based in Ibusuki, Kagoshima, Japan.

== Current Roles

* *Vice-President*, EXPRESS Language Foundation
* *CEO*, SATS Inc. (since 2020) — supporting international standardization
* *Deputy Convenor*, ISO TC 184/SC 4/WG 25 (since 2022)
* *Convenor*, ISO TC 184/SC 4/JWG 24 (since 2018)

== Previous Roles

* *Research Fellow*, Toshiba Research Corporation (2013–2018) — research and
  consulting for Toshiba Corporation researchers
* *Fellow*, Toshiba Corporation (2018–2020) — R&D center, specializing in
  product data and shape design
* *Chief Research Scientist*, Toshiba R&D center
* *Chair*, IEC SC 3D (2008–2017) — authored IEC 61360-2, IEC 62656-1, and
  IEC 62656-5
* *Expert*, ISO TC 184/SC4 (since 1996) — WG2, WG3, and JWG24

== Academic Background

From late 1989 to early 1991, Hiroshi studied computational geometry at École
des Mines de Saint-Étienne in France, under Prof. B. Peroche with academic
advisory from Dr. Dominique Michelucci. He worked closely with Prof. Guy
Pierra and his laboratory in Poitiers until Prof. Pierra's passing in 2012.

His technical specialties span nuclear engineering, shape design, anti-seismic
design of civil infrastructure, product data dictionaries and ontology, and
data modelling.
