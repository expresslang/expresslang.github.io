== Vice-President, EXPRESS Language Foundation

Hiroshi Murayama is a founding member and current Vice-President of the EXPRESS
Language Foundation, contributing to the organization's international outreach and
technical coordination.

== Contributions

Murayama has been involved in the EXPRESS and STEP standardization community,
contributing to the development and promotion of ISO 10303 standards. His work
spans both the technical and organizational aspects of the EXPRESS ecosystem.

== At ELF

* Vice-President of the EXPRESS Language Foundation
* Founding member of the organization
* International coordination and outreach
